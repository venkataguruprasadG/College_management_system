Frequently Asked Questions

How is the pricing calculated?
Our pricing is based primarily on the number of active student profiles you manage, allowing us to scale the required infrastructure and support efficiently. Additional fees may apply for certain third-party integrations or custom development work.
Is there a discount for annual billing?
Yes, all users who opt for annual billing receive a **15% discount** compared to paying month-to-month. This is reflected immediately when you select the 'Billed Annually' option above.
What happens if we exceed the student limit?
We will notify you when you approach your plan's student profile limit. You will have the option to upgrade to the next tier or request a customized add-on package to accommodate your growth without disruption.
