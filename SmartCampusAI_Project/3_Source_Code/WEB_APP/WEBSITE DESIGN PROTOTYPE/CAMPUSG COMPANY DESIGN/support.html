Account & Access

How do I reset my password?
Use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page and follow the emailed instructions. If the email doesn't arrive, check your spam folder.
How to change my user role?
Role changes (e.g., Student to Faculty) must be approved by a **Campus Administrator**. Please contact your internal college admin office.

Academic Management

Where can I upload class material?
Materials should be uploaded in the **Course Content** section within the specific course module you are teaching.
How to generate a final report card?
Final report cards are generated by the system after all grades are submitted and approved by the department head in the **Grading Module**.

Billing & Finance

How do I process a refund?
Refunds must be initiated via the **Finance Module** > **Payment Gateway** section. Note that processing times may vary.
Where are our invoices kept?
Invoices are available under the **Administration Dashboard** > **Subscription & Billing** tab.
